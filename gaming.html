Better Home Wi-Fi

Gaming performance depends on stability, not just speed.

Lower latency, fewer drops, and consistent connections matter more than raw bandwidth when gaming online.

What actually impacts gaming performance

  • Latency: How fast your connection responds
  • Packet loss: Dropped data causes lag spikes
  • Interference: Nearby networks disrupt stability
  • Device load: More devices = more competition

Best setup practices

  • Use wired Ethernet whenever possible
  • Use 5 GHz for lower latency
  • Keep router close to gaming device
  • Avoid congested channels
